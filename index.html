LifeDB by avsm

LifeDB server to manage personal data

The LifeDB is a schemaless P2P database designed to manage personal data. It has a collection of plugins which can input and output data to and from a variety of sources (e.g. IMAP, Twitter, Facebook, iPhoto) into the LifeDB. The idea is that you run the LifeDB on your own computers, and it can help you synchronize and transcode data between your personal devices and cloud services. Unlike most centralized solutions, this helps preserve your privacy since you can choose what goes public and what stays local.
